About Us

The Anandian Astronomical Association, in 1998, kindled curiosity about the cosmos, leaving an indelible mark on students across the island. Through its initiatives, the association organizes the annual Star Party and the Sir Arthur C. Clarke Memorial Challenge Trophy.
