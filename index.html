About Us

The Anandian Astronomical Association, In 1998, the Anandian Astronomical Association kindled curiosity about the cosmos, leaving an indelible mark on students across the island. Through its impactful initiatives, the association has organized the annual Star Party, a celestial celebration that captivates stargazers of all ages. Additionally, it orchestrates the Sir Arthur C. Clarke Memorial Challenge Trophy, an interschool quiz competition that fuels astronomical knowledge.
